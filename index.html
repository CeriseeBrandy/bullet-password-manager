Security & Confidentiality

🔐 LOCAL STORAGE

All your data is stored locally on your device.
No information is sent over the internet.

🔍 LEAK CHECK

Passwords are verified via Have I Been Pwned API.

⚠️ Only a part of the hash is sent (k-anonymity).
Your full password is never transmitted.

⚠️ LIMITES

This check only indicates whether a password has already been seen in leaks.
It does not allow you to know if your personal account has been compromised.
